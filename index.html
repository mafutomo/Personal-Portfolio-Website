E/PRO
Video Demo GitHub Repo

Technologies: React Native, Native Base, D3.js, Node.js, Knex.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL, Bcrypt, JSX, JavaScript, jQuery

E/Pro, a training and nutrition app that analyzes a woman’s unique cycle and birth control method to provide tailored nutrition and fitness guidance.

The app breaks down each woman’s cycle into four distinct phases (Performace, High-Intensity & Resistance, Aerobic, and Rest) based on fluctuating estrogen and progesterone levels.

With this information users can better understand their own physiology and use the app to create workout plans based on where they are in their cycle.

Live Site GitHub Repo

Technologies: Google API, Mocha, Chai, Supertest, Node.js, Express, Bcrypt, Knex.js, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Materialize, PostgreSQL.

With MappyHour users can view Boulder Happy Hour deals through our interactive map and intuitive interface. Once logged in, bookmark multiple restaurants and rate them.

Users can filter the map to see what Happy Hours are available on specific days or browse through restaurant descriptions, deals, and their contact information.
